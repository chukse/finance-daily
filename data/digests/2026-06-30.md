## Stories Worth Knowing

- **Samsung + SK Hynix: $520B chip commitment.** South Korea's two memory giants announced a combined $520B domestic chip-plant investment, the largest single-country semiconductor capex pledge on record. Structurally bullish for AI hardware supply chains.

- **Medicare to cover obesity drugs.** First-time GLP-1 coverage under Medicare begins — a major shift in drug reimbursement that expands the addressable market for Novo Nordisk and Eli Lilly meaningfully.

- **AeroVironment +28%.** Revenue and backlog both roughly doubled; backlog hit $1.2B. Defense drone demand accelerating as Trump's defense budget reshuffles procurement priorities.

- **Joby + Toyota JV.** The two companies announced a joint venture for air taxi production — Toyota brings manufacturing scale to Joby's FAA-certified eVTOL program.

- **Eurozone inflation cooler than expected.** The bloc's largest economies posted below-forecast CPI, raising ECB cut expectations and weakening the euro relative to the dollar.
