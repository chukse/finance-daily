## What Moved Markets

**Equities — broad rally, growth outperforms**
Russell 2000 led all major indices (+2.12%), followed by Nasdaq (+1.91%) and S&P 500 (+1.08%); the Dow lagged sharply (+0.14%), reflecting the tech/growth tilt. **Rates — mild headwind**
10Y Treasury yield held at 4.45%. WSJ flagged "hawkish new Fed" rhetoric as driving yield pressure; CNBC noted bond markets are now a key variable for AI infrastructure spending. No immediate rate cut catalyst visible.

**Gold — notable reversal**
Gold fell $51 to $4,172 (-1.21%), the mirror image of the Iran deal reducing flight-to-safety flows.

**Dollar — flat**
DX essentially unchanged at 100.85; no meaningful FX driver today.
