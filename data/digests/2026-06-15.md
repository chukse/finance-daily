**Rates & dollar:** 10Y yield slid 4 bps to 4.47% — traders repricing Fed hike risk lower as cheaper energy reduces inflation pressure. Dollar flat at 99.59.

**Gold:** +3.2% to $4,349 — unusual to rally alongside equities; likely reflecting continued dollar skepticism and geopolitical uncertainty residue.

**Chips:** The two-sided catalyst (Iran deal easing supply chain fears + ongoing AI demand) sent semis surging. MarketWatch flagged "two big geopolitical developments" driving the move.
